How to calculate your Jeepney fare?

  1. Know the Base Fare
    • Traditional Jeepneys – Usually ₱13.00 for the first 4 kilometers
    • Modern Jeepneys – Usually ₱14.00 for the first 4 kilometers
  2. Know the Additional Fare
    • Traditional Jeepneys - ₱1.80 - ₱2.00 per kilometer
    • Modern Jeepneys - ₱2.20 - ₱2.50 per kilometer
  3. Check for Fare Discounts
    • Students, senior citizens, and PWDs get a 20% discount
  4. Consider Special Cases
    • Late-night or special trips might have additional charges
    • Always bring small bills/coins as drivers may not always have change
